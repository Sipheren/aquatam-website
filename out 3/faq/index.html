Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about AWTS servicing, maintenance, and installations.

Your Aerated Wastewater Treatment System requires servicing every 90 days as per council and state water regulations. Regular servicing ensures optimal performance and extends the life of your system.

We service all AWTS units within a 300km radius of Tamworth. This includes Gunnedah, Narrabri, Wee Waa, Coonabarabran, Inverell, Moree, Armidale, Glen Innes, Muswellbrook, Scone, Singleton, Denman, Merriwa, and surrounding areas.

We service all major brands including Taylex, BioCycle, BioSeptic, Earthsafe, Krystal Kleer, Ozzie Kleen, Aquanova, and Garden Master.

Our AWTS servicing starts from $85 per service. Contact us for a specific quote based on your system type and location.

Yes, we offer free quotes on new tank installations. We use Taylex Plastic tanks and Taylex Concrete tanks depending on your area and specific requirements.

During a service, we inspect all components, check water quality, clean filters, test the aerator and pump, and ensure the system is operating correctly. We provide a report after each service.
